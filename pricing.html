Ways in protecting the ozone layer

I

Refrain from using hazardous gases such as CFCs, halogenated hydrocarbon, methyl bromide and nitrous oxide due to their manufacturing process and contents.

II

Minimize the use of personal cars, and instead use public transport, bicycles, or walk whenever possible. In case you need to use a car, try carpooling with others to reduce the number of cars on the road, and thereby decrease pollution levels.

III

Avoid cleaning products that are harmful to both the environment and health, as many contain corrosive substances and solvents. Opt for non-toxic substitutes like vinegar or bicarbonate instead.

IV

Support the use of local products, as this reduces the distance traveled by food, and subsequently decreases the amount of nitrous oxide emitted during transportation.
